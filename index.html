About Me

My name is Kojin Glick, and I'm a web developer with a constant passion for learning. In 2019, I graduated from UC Berkeley with a major in both Media Studies and Political Science. Not content to leave learning behind, I enrolled in the Full-Stack Flex program at the UC Berkeley extension. There, I gained fluency in the MERN stack (Mongo DB, Express, React, and Node.js). I know Python and Javascript, as well as MySQL as an alternative to Mongo DB.

In the space between learning and practicing my full-stack development skills, I enjoy writing short stories, backpacking, and keeping up with the latest memes.
